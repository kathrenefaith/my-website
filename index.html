Hi there!

I’m a front-end web developer with a passion for storytelling. That means I'm committed to the flow, the details, and the overarching narrative of what you bring to the table -- whether that's a business, a brand, a blog (you name it!) -- and how that translates into your online presence. I create elegantly coded and well-developed websites that make your story inviting and stand out from the rest. Allow me to help you tell it.

Below are the skills I offer.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting PSD to HTML

You can trust me to take a designer’s PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
